Solidarity Sans

Solidarity Sans is a display typeface inspired by Cuban and Constructivist typography.

It is rigid, geometric, and architectural, evoking the contours of Brutalist forms while embracing a subtle off-kilterness informed by the tropics.

Designed with play in mind, it is simple enough to be cut apart and reconfigured, yet complex enough to hold its own among other block type.
